About me

Short Bio

Hello everyone! My name is Sajjad Hajian from Iran. I was born in 1996. I love learning about new and confusing topics. So my favorite field is Complex systems!

BSc

I studied and graduated BSc in condensed matter physics from 2016 to 2019 at Kashan University (Kashan-Iran).

MSc

I studied and graduated MSc in complex systems from 2019 to 2022 at the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS) (Zanjan-Iran). I defended my master's thesis with the title The effect of people's variability in accepting behavioral patterns on disease control.
  My supervisor was Dr. Nahid Azimi.

RA

Now I research Simplicial models of social contagion at the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences (IASBS). Also, I like to learn more about hypergraphs.
